About Us

Dependable Tree Care Built Around Clean Work

81Tree Services serves Tampa Bay with tree removal, trimming, storm cleanup, stump grinding, land clearing, palm service, and debris hauling.

Talk With the Team

Tree service with a clear plan

81Tree Services focuses on the parts of tree care property owners actually feel: whether the crew arrives prepared, whether the work is explained clearly, whether surrounding property is respected, and whether the site is clean when the job is complete.

The team handles routine trimming and palm work, but also larger removals, storm cleanup, land clearing, stump grinding, debris hauling, and emergency tree situations across Tampa Bay.

What customers consistently mention

Clean job sites

Public reviews repeatedly point to organized work areas, strong cleanup, and properties that are easier to use after service.

Clear communication

Emergency readiness

The business accepts urgent calls for storm damage, fallen limbs, hazardous trees, and blocked access.

Community discount

81Tree Services advertises a 10% discount for veterans and first responders.

How the work is approached

Every property is different. A tight South Tampa lot, a large Lutz yard, a Brandon palm cleanup, and a commercial removal all need different staging, equipment, and cleanup planning. The common thread is simple: understand the site, plan the work, handle the tree safely, and leave the property clean.

That planning matters most around structures, utilities, gates, fences, driveways, irrigation, lighting, and landscaping. The more clearly those details are handled before cutting begins, the smoother the job feels for the property owner.
